5 INTERIOR DESIGN TRENDS OF 2024 | 2024 SOFA TREND

Favored for their soft and relaxing design, sofas have become an essential element of modern interiors. In 2024 - a year of fresh and cozy trend - how will sofa choices evolve? Join EVOCASA to explore the top interior trends of 2024.

1. Modular Sofa Remains The Leading Trend

Always aiming for tidiness and flexibility in arrangement, modular sofa designs continue to be a top favorite in 2024, becoming one of the most loved trends for modern living rooms in recent years. With detachable modules, you can easily customize the layout of your sofa in various configurations, such as L-shaped, U-shaped, or even as separate pieces for different rooms. This makes them ideal for spaces of all sizes and usage needs. Beyond just flexible arrangements, modular sofas enhance interaction among family members and create an open, welcoming atmosphere.
Most modular sofas feature sleek, minimalist designs, making them effortlessly adaptable to various interior styles, from Modern, Minimalist, Scandinavian, to Neo-Classical. At EVOCASA, you can explore a wide range of modular sofas such as BOLD, ABBEY, DELIGHT, ALTHEA, and more each with unique designs and refined details.

2. Classic Velvet

Velvet is predicted to be the standout material of 2024 due to its fashionable appeal and elegance. As a soft fabric with natural water resistance and easy-to-clean properties, velvet upholstery offers a luxurious and comfortable seating experience. Therefore, for rooms designed with a cozy and sophisticated ambiance, a velvet sofa is an excellent choice.
Additionally, pairing velvet sofas with silk or linen throws and cushions in light tones or smoky gray will create a more harmonious and refreshing space. According to interior designers, 2024 is expected to mark a transformation in design trends, with velvet sofas combined with contrasting-colored throws and cushions, bringing a fresh and youthful aesthetic to modern interiors.

3. Almost peach

As the color of 2024, peachy pink is highly favored and widely used in makeup, fashion, and even interior design. This shade exudes the warmth of a sunny summer day, making it a perfect choice for creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere. A peachy pink sofa adds comfort and softness to your space without feeling dull or monotonous. When paired with neutral-toned cushions and throws, the room achieves a sense of balance and harmony. You can also combine peachy pink with pastel hues leaning towards coral, moss green, or navy blue to create accents, adding a youthful and eye-catching touch to your interior

4. Embracing Minimalism

Not a new trend, minimalism has remained a favorite in recent years due to its clean aesthetic and the sense of tranquility it brings. Following the principle of "Less is more," minimalist design focuses on simplicity and refinement.

This style often features sofas in neutral tones, particularly white, cream, and gray, with sleek, squared-off lines for an elegant touch. A key advantage of minimalism is the use of natural light, which enhances the brightness and openness of a space.

Minimalist sofas are typically adorned with simple, light-colored cushions and throws, paired with monochrome coffee tables for a cohesive look. To prevent the space from feeling too plain, you can add textured knit cushions with subtle patterns to bring warmth and visual interest to the design.

5. Earthy Tones

Primarily blending the freshness of green with the depth of brown, nature-inspired tones bring a sense of calm and relaxation. Unlike the bold vibrancy of red or yellow, and not as simple as black and white, natural hues offer a perfect balance for spaces seeking a refresh without compromising elegance.
A green sofa paired with a wooden coffee table, complemented by indoor plants, creates a harmonious and sophisticated aesthetic, making your living space both inviting and stylish.A combination of earthy tones and natural textures brings warmth to the space, creating a perfect environment for relaxation. The addition of light fixtures with soft illumination further enhances the cozy ambiance, ensuring that every corner feels inviting.
