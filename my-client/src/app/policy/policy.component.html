RETURN POLICY

At Evocasa, we take pride in the quality of our products and strive to maintain the highest standards in service. We aim to minimize any errors that might affect your shopping experience. However, should an issue arise, we offer an efficient returns and replacements process.The return policy applies to customers who shop and make payments on the EvoCasa website.

Condition For Return

  • Products sold are non-returnable unless a defect is identified at the time of purchase
  • For orders purchased in-store: A one-time return is applicable for full-priced products within 7 days from the date stated on the receipt.
  • For orders purchased on the website:
    • A one-time return is applicable for full-priced products and discounted products (up to 30% off) within 14 days from the date the customer receives the product.
    • A one-time return is applicable for products discounted more than 30% within 5 days from the date the customer receives the product. Only size returns (if available) are permitted, and style returns are not allowed.
  • Note: The above return/return periods are calculated from the date the customer receives the product, as shown in the courier's system. Customers must initiate their return/return request and send back the product within the specified timeframe.
  • The original purchase receipt (or a copy in the case of partial returns) must be provided.
  • All returned products must be new, unused, and retain original tags, packaging, and accompanying accessories as initially provided (including product inserts) along with bags, boxes, and any complimentary gifts (if applicable).
  • Returns are only accepted for products of equal or higher value than the original purchase price:
    • If the returned product's value is higher, customers must pay the price difference.
    • If the returned product's value is lower, EvoCasa will not refund the difference.
  • The return policy does not apply to custom-made orders.

Note: If the product does not meet the conditions outlined in this return Policy, the return request will be denied, and the product will be returned to the customer at their expense. For convenience, customers are advised to check the product condition before initiating a return request.

WARRANTY POLICY

This warranty policy applies to customers shopping and making payments directly at EvoCasa store systems or via the EvoCasa website.

Warranty Period:

The warranty period is calculated from the purchase date (based on the purchase receipt).

  • Home decor items: 12 months
  • Furniture: 24 months

Warranty Conditions:

  • The product must still be within the warranty period.
  • A valid purchase receipt must be presented, containing complete information and without being torn, damaged, or tampered with.

Cases Where Warranty Is Denied:

  • The product does not have a valid purchase receipt.
  • The warranty period has expired.
  • Discounted products according to EvoCasa’s sales policies.
  • Products with scratches, damages caused by the customer during usage.
  • Damages resulting from improper use not following EvoCasa’s usage instructions.
  • Products altered or repaired by non-EvoCasa personnel.
  • Damages caused by insects or animals.
  • Damages due to force majeure events such as natural disasters, fires, earthquakes, etc.

Warranty Scope

  • EvoCasa only provides warranties for products damaged due to manufacturing defects or material faults.
  • Home decor items: Warranty covers visible defects such as misaligned joints, discoloration, accessory oxidation, or detachment of lacquered accessories due to insufficient technical handling.

Note: If the product does not meet the conditions outlined in the Warranty Policy, the warranty request will be denied, and the product will be returned to the customer at their expense. For convenience, please check the product’s condition before submitting a warranty request.

PRIVACY POLICY

Personal Statement

We are committed to creating a secure and user-friendly experience for every customer. To achieve this, we aim to be as clear as possible about all our policies, as is evident by our transparent Terms and Conditions.When you visit our site, some of your personal information supplied during your order or by means of our cookies policy may be stored for future use.

What are "Cookies"?

Cookies are little text files that are stored within your browser's cache. First and third-party cookies are used on this site for functionality, statistics, and advertising.

Why do we use cookies?

Cookies is what keep track of settings, thus allowing your shopping experience to be tailored to you (remembering your preferences or settings).Cookies also gather data, for example, the time of a session, viewed pages, or just internal demographic information, like age and gender. The information that is gathered can then be used on-site for advertising purposes, which helps us create customized shopping experiences for our users. We do not use cookies that will track or identify you.

What information do we gather specifically?

The information we gather is what you supply us with when signing up for a newsletter or making a purchase. This is usually demographic information like name, address, and email, or general contact information. Cookies will also gather session information like the pages you visited, the amount of time spent on each, location, transactions, and any other general browsing behavior.

What third-parties do we share your information with?

Any information collected and stored will only be shared with our affiliate partners for analytical reasons. We will not share your personal information past our brand and trusted brand partners.

SHIPPING POLICY

General Provisions:

  • Shipping carriers will not resolve compensation claims if you accept the package and sign the delivery receipt. Therefore, please carefully inspect the product's packaging before confirming receipt. If the packaging shows signs of damage (tears, dents, holes, or water damage) and you suspect that the goods inside are damaged, kindly refuse to accept the delivery and inform us immediately.
  • Orders will be delivered to your doorstep, except in cases such as restricted access to office areas or high-rise apartments (delivery will only be made to the building's main entrance).
  • We encourage customers to record a video of the unboxing process to document the condition of the packaging and its contents. This will serve as evidence for handling complaints (if necessary).
  • For any complaints related to your order, please provide feedback to Hanoia with complete order details, including images of the package's current state and product photos, so we can promptly resolve the issue.

Delivery Coverage

  • EvoCasa offers nationwide shipping within Vietnam. For international shipping outside of Vietnam, please contact EvoCasa's Customer Support Department before placing your order.
  • In case of pandemics or unforeseen circumstances at the time of delivery, the shipping areas may be adjusted according to government regulations.

Delivery Time:

Shipping services operate during business hours from Monday to Saturday (excluding Sundays and public holidays as per state regulations).

  • Express Delivery: 2–5 business days.
  • Store Pickup: Within 24–72 hours.

Note: The delivery times mentioned above are estimated and may change due to unforeseen circumstances. Customers will be informed of any changes. Delivery times for remote areas, island districts, or special administrative zones may require additional 1–3 days compared to standard delivery times.
