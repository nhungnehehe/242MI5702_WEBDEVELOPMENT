DELIVERY METHOD

ClayCo offers reliable nationwide door-to-door delivery service across Vietnam, ensuring your purchases reach you securely and on time.

I. Shipping Fees

  • Ho Chi Minh City: Shipping within Ho Chi Minh City is charged at a standard rate of 20,000 VND.
  • Other Locations: For destinations outside Ho Chi Minh City, the shipping fee is set at 30,000 VND. This fee applies uniformly to all locations within Vietnam, regardless of distance.

II. Delivery Time

  • Ho Chi Minh City: Orders are typically delivered within 1-2 working days. Note that deliveries are not made on Sundays or public holidays.
  • Other Locations: For deliveries to other provinces and cities, the estimated delivery time is 2-4 working days, excluding Sundays and public holidays. Certain remote areas may experience slightly longer delivery times.

If you are unavailable to receive your package, our delivery partner will arrange a convenient time for redelivery at no additional cost. Our aim is to make the delivery process as seamless as possible for our customers.

At ClayCo, we take pride in the quality of our products and strive to maintain the highest standards in service. We aim to minimize any errors that might affect your shopping experience. However, should an issue arise, we offer an efficient returns and replacements process.

I. Return and Exchange Conditions

Customers may request a return or exchange under the following conditions:

  • The product received does not match the quality, description, or specifications as shown on the website.
  • The item delivered is of a different model, color, or variation than what was ordered.
  • The product exhibits clear technical defects originating from the manufacturing process, impacting its function or appearance.

Important Note: Customers must notify us within 7 days of receiving the product if they wish to initiate a return or exchange. Requests submitted after this period may not be eligible for processing.

II. Return and Exchange Procedure

To initiate a return or exchange:

  • Contact our Hotline: Please call our customer service hotline at 19001052. Have your order details ready to help us address your concern quickly.
  • Issue Verification: Our customer service team will verify the issue and confirm the return or exchange eligibility.
  • Free Return or Exchange: Upon verification, we will arrange a free pickup of the item for return or replacement, and promptly process the exchange or refund, depending on your preference.

We value your satisfaction and will do our utmost to resolve any concerns in a timely and professional manner. Thank you for choosing ClayCo!

30-DAY GUARANTEE

We guarantee your plants will arrive in happy, healthy condition. If not, we replace them for free as part of our30-Day Customer Happiness Guarantee. It’s natural for live plants to show minor signs of stress after transit but if the damage is beyond minor blemishes*, reach out to us within 30 days of receipt to receive a free replacement.

The guarantee applies to all online and in-store purchases within the first 30 days. We require photos as proof of damage and to help us improve our packaging process. You can also bring an online order into any of our shops for a replacement. Inventory online and in-store differs, so we recommend calling your closest shop in advance to ensure availability.

*Remember, it’s natural for live plants to show minor signs of stress after transit. You can help your plant perk up from its journey by gently pruning any foliage damaged during the shipping process, such as bent stems or yellow leaves. Removing damaged foliage gives your plant back energy for new, healthy growth.

What are "Cookies"?

Cookies are little text files that are stored within your browser's cache. First and third-party cookies are used on this site for functionality, statistics, and advertising.

Why do we use cookies?

Cookies is what keep track of settings, thus allowing your shopping experience to be tailored to you (remembering your preferences or settings).Cookies also gather data, for example, the time of a session, viewed pages, or just internal demographic information, like age and gender. The information that is gathered can then be used on-site for advertising purposes, which helps us create customized shopping experiences for our users. We do not use cookies that will track or identify you.

What information do we gather specifically?

The information we gather is what you supply us with when signing up for a newsletter or making a purchase. This is usually demographic information like name, address, and email, or general contact information. Cookies will also gather session information like the pages you visited, the amount of time spent on each, location, transactions, and any other general browsing behavior.

What third-parties do we share your information with?

Any information collected and stored will only be shared with our affiliate partners for analytical reasons. We will not share your personal information past our brand and trusted brand partners.
