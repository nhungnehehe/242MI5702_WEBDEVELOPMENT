Introducing Our One-Year Anniversary Collection: "Eternal Nostalgia"

For EvoCasa’s 1-year anniversary collection, “Eternal Nostalgia,” we celebrate the beautiful fusion of vintage charm and contemporary sophistication. This collection highlights the transformative journey of materials, breathing new life into antique and reclaimed elements.

Our collection highlighted pieces are the Arnt Lande Chair designed by Arnt Lande and produced by Møller & Stokke during the early 1940s, these chairs epitomize the era’s emphasis on simplicity and high-quality materials. Constructed from pine—a local wood known for its strength and beauty—these chairs exhibit masterful craftsmanship. The gorgeous Sea Fish, c.1870 vintage reprint from the famous artist, Antoine Vollon, a distinguished French painter celebrated for his exceptional still-life compositions and genre scenes. Seen in Musée d'Orsay. This collection features exquisite, distinct pieces that are ready to elevate your home or your clients’ spaces with unparalleled elegance.

Thank you to everyone who has joined us on this journey and supported us through our first successful year!
